Introduction

Welcome to my website. While it is almost impossible to describe all the great experiences I had in the course of the one year spend in LDT, I will share a few that transformed me the most. I have divided these into four categories and here is an overview of each:

  • Final Project:  You will find a description of my final project - The Mathic Touch

  • Other Projects:  Most classes were project based. Three of these, two involving application development and another consisting cross-cultural design with Chinese students are described.

  • Course Highlights:  I took over twenty classes and provide highlights of almost half of them.

  • About Me:  Who am I and how did I get here? Learn some information you will neither find in my resume nor the social networking sites.

This site primarily hosts a portfolio of my work completed during the Learning, Design & Technology program at Stanford University. You will also find additional information about me as well as links to resources of interest to learning and technology designers.
